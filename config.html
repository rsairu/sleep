Remaining wake time thresholds

Set when the app switches between Active, Winding down, and Pre-sleep. Percentages are your share of wake time left from your recent 7-day average get-up time to average fell-asleep time—the same window the header clock uses. The times under each slider show roughly when those boundaries fall on a typical day.
