How strong is a daily flag?

The flag color is how far that measure drifted from your previous seven logged nights. (Fewer than 7 prior nights in the log will show an insufficient-data message.)

  • Slightly off pattern.
  • Moderately off pattern.
  • Severely off pattern.
😴 Asleep is only flagged when you fell asleep later than your baseline; falling asleep earlier is not flagged. ⌛ Duration uses the worse of two checks (not added together): shorter than your recent average, or absolute short sleep—under 6 hours (slight), under 5 hours (moderate), under 4 hours (severe)—based on total sleep including naps. 🌅 Wake compares earlier or later than the baseline. 🧩 WASO (wake after sleep onset) counts wake episodes longer than 20 minutes: 1 → slight, 2 → moderate, 3 or more → severe. On the history calendar, the cell color is the worst of those severities among asleep, duration, and WASO.
