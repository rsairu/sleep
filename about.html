Remaining Wake Time

Shows how much time is left in your day before your recommended sleep time. Phases are based on the percent of wake time remaining (these thresholds may become configurable in a future update).

The day is divided into three simple phases:

  • ☀️
    1. “Open time” (plenty of runway) Range: ~100% → 40% remaining (i.e., you’re in the first ~60% of your day) Feeling: steady, normal, no urgency
  • 🌇
    2. “Winding down window” Range: ~40% → 15% remaining This is the “you’re past the midpoint” zone Feeling: awareness, gentle shift
  • 🛏️
    3. “Pre-sleep phase” Range: ~15% → 0% remaining Time to start easing out and prepare for rest

This is meant to gently guide your pace, not rush you.
